Our dedicated design studio in Claremont is now open

Located at our Claremont branch office, Addstyle has opened it’s new Design Studio.

This inspiring, creative space is dedicated to all things design, from architectural services, including large-screen 3D walk-throughs of your home extension concept, to a comprehensive interior design consultancy.

Our interior designer, is available to advise and guide clients through everything from construction selections, to colour consultancy, furniture, rugs, lighting and styling. A place where clients can ensure every aspect of their home renovation is expertly curated.

We're excited to now offer complimentary, personalised interior design consultations with Lisa, to all Addstyle clients.
